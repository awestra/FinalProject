One of the Earth's most beautiful creatures is in danger. We need to save them.

Make a Difference




Giraffes are Essential.

Giraffes help shape and maintain important ecosystems. Without them, balance would be lost. Look at the programs below to learn about organizations fighting to save giraffes, or contact us to place a donation in order to make a difference. Even by taking time to research and grow to love giraffes you help our efforts to keep these magestic creatures here with us. Learn about the impact of giraffes and their relationship with both humans and their habitats.





Looking for a Brighter Future

Many organizations and teams are working to ensure that the next generation can live in a world with giraffes. Efforts include protecting essential habitats, conducting research on giraffe genetics to preserve the species, and fighting against illegal poaching to preserve the safety and livelihood of our animal friends.

The Giraffe Conservation Foundation

An international organization, the Giraffe Conservation Foundation works in 15 African countries and helps manage 45 million acres of giraffe habitat. They work with local African communities to relocate giraffes to areas where they have been extinct for generations.

Save Giraffes Now

Another international organization, Save Giraffes Now helps participate in conservation efforts and applies donations and funding to help giraffes. They run anti-poaching campaigns, along with relocation efforts and shelters for young orphaned giraffes.

The Masai Giraffe Project

Part of the Wild Nature Institute, the Masai Giraffe Project works to raise awareness for the Masai giraffe with grassroots projects and education initiatives.
